Note About Species

Your character's species provides plenty of clues as to the sort of person he or she is, how he or she feels about characters of other species and what motivates him or her.

Remember, however, that the species descriptions only apply to the mojority of people. In each species, some individuals diverge from the norm, and you character can be one of these.

Extra Languages

Characters with an Intelligence bonus know how to speak, read, and write other languages as well. For each point of Intelligence bonus, a character can speak, read, and write one additional language of his or her choice.
